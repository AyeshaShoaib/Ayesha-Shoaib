"let me take you on my Art & Fashion Journey "

My journey with art started so many years ago. As a kid, I loved colors and all the beautiful colored images on my schoolbooks and storybooks.
Everyday is an opportunity to take one more step on your path to fulfilling your dream of become a fashion designer.
I design from my experiences. I feel you find beauty in every step of your journey and you turn that into fashion, you wear it and carry it as your own masterpiece. You are the canvas and your style is the creation.
Clothing is such an expression of individuality, I love researching different era's and time periods, strong women in our history. I want to design clothing that makes women feel like they can express themselves.
My mom She did beautiful hand embroideries. Her color choices were always so vibrant and fun. She had great personal style in Fashion and Home.
These days, the love for fashion and fashion design among youngsters has increased way more than in the last few decades.
As a fashion designer, you'll research current fashion trends, forecasting what will be popular with consumers, and take inspiration from the world around you to create fresh and original designs.
how to learn Fashion Designing? Study and keep studying  Keep an eye on skill development Focus your efforts 
Thank you for reading my article. I hope you guys like it.
